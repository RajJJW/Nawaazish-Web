Why was Nawaazish founded?

Nawaazish was founded with the noble aim to foster change in our society.

"The smallest act of kindness is worth more than the greatest intentions." We the members of Nawaazish stand by this thought.

We are an organization driven by change. A change that we want in society, for us and the people around us. We aim to create awareness and educate our society, one day & one person at a time.

Our Mission

We here at Nawaazish Crew work with the aim to create a society of equal opportunities and an environment that sustains one and all. We believe in the idealogy that all life is equal and put everything in making this idealogy a ground reality for everyone.

About Us

We are Nawaazish crew - a group of college students from different realms of life, on a mission to contribute to a society of equal opportunities, an environment that sustains one and all.
